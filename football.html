James Madison Football

Bridgeforth Stadium/Zane Showker Field is home to the James Madison University football Dukes. The facility was built in 1975, enlarged in 1981, and in 2011 completed a major $62 million renovation that increased seating capacity to 24,877, added multiple amenities and made it one of the premier facilities to accompany what is already one of the best game day atmospheres among college football peer institutions. James Madison finished their first season competing in the Sunbelt with a confrence record of 6-2 and an overall record of 8-3.
